## Supreme Court Daily Digest [22 January 2026]

<h3 id="hemalatha-d-v-tukaram-d-2026-insc-82registered-sale-deeddeclaration-as-sham-documentpresumption-about-validity-genuineness"><a href="https://www.caseciter.com/content/files/2026/01/Hemalatha--D---v.-Tukaram--D---2026-INSC-82---Registered-Sale-Deed---Declaration-As-Sham-Document---Presumption-About-Validity---Genuineness.pdf"><strong>Hemalatha (D) v.
