 George Dominic  .v. Kanjirapilly Service Cooperative Bank Ltd and others What are the principles for preventing abuse of Judicial process ?
Practise—Constructive res judicata—Henderson Principle—All claims and issues that could and should have been raised in an earlier proceeding are barred from being raised in subsequent litigation, except in exceptional circumstances—Code of Civil Procedure, 1908 (Central Act 5 of 1908)—Section 11.
